Aaronson Oracle

An Aaronson Oracle is a concept introduced by the theoretical physicist and computer scientist Scott Aaronson. It refers to a hypothetical computational oracle that can predict the outcome of certain events or actions, based on statistical patterns or historical data.

In the context of this Color Prediction Oracle, the Aaronson Oracle attempts to predict the next color you will choose based on the frequency of your previous color choices. It uses a simple statistical model to make predictions, updating its model with each new user choice. The Correct Prediction Percentage provides insights into the performance of the prediction model.

Free Will Rating

The Free Will Rating is a measure of the perceived freedom in your color choices compared to the predictions made by the Aaronson Oracle.

It is calculated as follows: For every percentage point the Correct Prediction Percentage is above or below 50%, the Free Will Rating decreases by 2%. A higher Free Will Rating indicates a higher perceived freedom in your color choices.
